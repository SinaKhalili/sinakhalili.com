phi phenomenon

The phi phenomenon is the apparent perception of motion from the alternating flashing of stimuli. It looks like a shadow is going over the objects.

Related beta movement is the opposite effect, where the mind creates a movements from objects in the foreground.

Epistemic status: #idk
