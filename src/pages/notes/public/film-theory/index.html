film theory

When you write about film, it's called film theory. Related to Film analysis. I'm still not sure how much of a role it makes in filmmaking, but it's interesting nonetheless.

Types

Thre are two types of film theory: classical film theory | contemporary film theory

Note that contemporary is actually just anything after the 1960's, which is not really that contemporary. Lol.
