gaze

[ Category - Philosophy ]

The gaze is the perception or awareness with which you view something.

For example in film, you can have a "male gaze" which is framing shots in such a way to accentuate the sensual aspects of females, done for males to derive pleasure from.

See also: voyeurism (film).
